## Why People Look for bSafe Alternatives

bSafe has been downloaded over a million times, and the concept is genuinely good. But the reviews tell a different story:

- **Core features are paywalled** — users frequently report discovering that SOS, live streaming, and reliable tracking all require a paid subscription, often only when they try to use them
- **GPS reliability problems** — location data is sometimes off by entire neighborhoods, or stops updating entirely
- **Subscription billing complaints** — annual plans default without clear notice, and refunds are difficult to get
- **Reliability in emergencies** — the scenario where you need an app to work perfectly is exactly when you can't afford it to fail

The concept — hands-free SOS, live video streaming, evidence creation — is powerful. But for people who want a simple, dependable way to let someone know they got home safe, bSafe can feel like overkill that underdelivers.

CheckIn More isn't trying to replace a full emergency response system. It's built for the quieter version of personal safety: the daily reassurance that someone is watching out for you, and the on-demand safety net when you're heading somewhere alone.

Beyond one-off timers, CheckIn More lets you set a recurring daily check-in schedule — different times on different days, multiple check-ins per day, and a customizable window before contacts are notified. It's the ongoing safety net that bSafe doesn't offer.

CheckIn More doesn't request location permissions. Your contacts learn that you're safe (or that you haven't checked in) — not where you are at all times. For users who found bSafe's "always on" location tracking uncomfortable, this is a meaningful difference.

No. Your contacts receive alerts by email, SMS, or phone call. No account, no app required on their end.

No. CheckIn More doesn't request location permissions. The app is built around scheduled check-ins and timers, not GPS tracking.

Yes, on Plus and Premium plans. Start, extend, or cancel timers entirely over SMS.
