## Why People Look for IAmFine Alternatives

IAmFine takes a deliberately simple approach: one automated phone call per day, and if you don't answer, your Care Circle gets notified. That simplicity is genuinely valuable — especially for seniors who prefer a phone call to an app. But it creates real friction for users who want more:

- **One daily call, fixed to a single time** — no option for multiple check-ins or different schedules by day
- **No free plan** — $14.99/month is the only option
- **Passive check-in only** — there's no way to signal how you're actually feeling, or to proactively ask for help
- **Phone-centric design** — great if you're not a smartphone user, but limiting if you are
- **No scheduling flexibility** — you can change your call time, but can't set different times for different days

If any of those are friction points for you, CheckIn More is worth a close look.
