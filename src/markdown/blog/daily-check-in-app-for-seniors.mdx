As the developer behind CheckIn More, this idea started from something I noticed in my own family: seniors often reach a stage where they're too independent to move into senior living, but not quite independent enough to be left entirely on their own.

At that stage, family members feel a quiet responsibility to check in every day. A quick call or text becomes a routine: *"Just checking on you — do you need anything?"* These daily check-ins come from love. But when they happen out of obligation, they can start to feel like a chore — for everyone.

That's why I built CheckIn More: a **daily check-in app for seniors** designed to reduce the pressure on families while keeping seniors safe, supported, and independent.

---

## How It Works

Instead of requiring daily calls or texts, CheckIn More sends your loved one a simple notification at the times you choose. They check in with a tap. That's it.

If they miss a check-in, their trusted contacts are automatically notified — by email, text, or phone call — so you know when it actually matters to reach out.

No hovering. No guilt. Just quiet reassurance in the background.

---

## Why Families Choose CheckIn More

**It replaces obligation with automation.** The daily "are you okay?" call often isn't really a conversation — it's a status check. CheckIn More handles the status check automatically, so your real conversations can actually be meaningful.

**It respects your loved one's independence.** Seniors don't want to feel monitored. A simple daily tap — on their schedule, in their own home — feels nothing like surveillance. It feels like routine.

**It's flexible enough for real life.** Check-in times can be customized by day of the week. Seniors can pause check-ins for vacations or family visits. Contacts can be added or adjusted anytime.

**Contacts don't need an app.** Family members receive alerts by email, text, or phone call — no account or download required.

## Who It's For

CheckIn More is a great fit for seniors who:

- Live alone and want a simple daily safety net
- Value their independence but understand the peace of mind it gives family
- Have loved ones who worry but don't want to be intrusive
- Are recovering from surgery or managing a health condition

It's also built for **adult children** who want to stay informed without turning every morning into a check-up call.

## A Note on What This Isn't

CheckIn More is a **daily wellness check-in app** — not a medical alert device. It doesn't have a wearable button or GPS tracking. If your loved one needs emergency response capabilities, a service like Life Alert pairs well alongside CheckIn More.

Yes. Check-in times can be set for specific days of the week, and seniors can pause check-ins anytime their routine changes.
