**CheckIn More** is a simple, reliable **daily check-in app** designed for people who live alone and the families who care about them.

With customizable check-in times and instant alerts if a check-in is missed, the app helps ensure safety without constant phone calls or disruptions. Loved ones get reassurance, while users maintain their freedom and privacy.

Whether you're supporting an elderly parent, a family member living alone, or simply want extra peace of mind, CheckIn More makes daily wellness check-ins effortless and dependable.
