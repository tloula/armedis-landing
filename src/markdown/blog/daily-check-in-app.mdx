A **daily check-in app** does something simple but powerful: it lets the people who care about you know you're okay — automatically, every day, without a phone call.

If you or someone you love lives alone, you already know the quiet worry that comes with it. A daily check-in app turns that worry into a routine — and a routine into peace of mind.

---

## How a Daily Check-In App Works

The concept is straightforward:

1. You set a time to check in each day
2. The app sends you a reminder when it's time
3. You check in with a tap
4. If you miss your check-in, your designated contacts are automatically notified

No one has to wonder. No one has to make an awkward daily phone call. The app handles it quietly in the background.

Daily check-in apps are most commonly used by:

---

## What to Look for in a Daily Check-In App

**Flexible scheduling.** The best apps let you set different check-in times for different days — and pause when your routine changes.

**Multiple alert methods.** Contacts should be reachable by email, text, or phone call depending on urgency and preference.

**Simple to use.** If checking in takes more than a few seconds, it won't stick.

**No app required for contacts.** Family members shouldn't need to download anything to receive alerts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is a daily check-in app the same as a medical alert device?**
No. A medical alert device like Life Alert responds to emergencies in the moment. A daily check-in app is for scheduled, routine wellness check-ins — it's a daily safety net, not an emergency button. Many people use both.

**Do my contacts need to download the app?**
No. Contacts receive alerts by email, text, or phone call — no account or app required.

**How is this different from just texting family every morning?**
A check-in app automates the safety net. If you forget to text — or can't — your contacts are alerted automatically. There's no relying on someone to notice a missed message.
