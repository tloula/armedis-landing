## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does CheckIn More work without a smartphone?**
CheckIn More requires the user to have a smartphone to send check-ins. However, your emergency contacts only need a phone that can receive texts or phone calls — no app, no account.

**How does CheckIn More notify contacts?**
Depending on your preferences: email, SMS text, or phone call. Contacts receive alerts for completed check-ins, missed check-ins, and assistance requests. You control which alerts go to which contacts.

**Is there a free plan?**
Yes, CheckIn More has a free plan with email notifications and  a **14-day free trial** on our paid plans adding text and phone call alerts.

**Can I pause my check-ins?**
Yes. You can easily disable check-ins for vacations or any time your routine changes, without canceling your account.

**How is CheckIn More different from a medical alert device?**
CheckIn More is a daily wellness check-in app, not a medical alert system. It doesn't have a wearable button or GPS tracking — it's designed for scheduled, routine check-ins that keep your loved ones informed.
