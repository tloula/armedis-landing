## The Key Differences

### <Icon name="target" /> Purpose

This is the most important difference. **Life360 is a family GPS tracker.** It's designed for parents who want to know where their kids are, monitor teen drivers, and coordinate family logistics. Everyone in the circle shares their location continuously.

**CheckIn More is a daily wellness check-in app.** It's designed for adults who live alone — or their loved ones who want reassurance — built around a simple scheduled ritual: are you okay today?

Same broad category of "family safety." Completely different products.

### <Icon name="map-pin-off" /> Location Tracking

Life360 requires "always on" location permissions and continuously shares your location with everyone in your Circle. That's central to what it does.

CheckIn More doesn't request location permissions at all. There's nothing to track between check-ins — it's entirely schedule-based.

### <Icon name="users" /> What Your Contacts Need

To receive alerts from Life360, your contacts need to download the app and join your Circle.

With CheckIn More, your contacts receive notifications by email, SMS, or phone call. No app. No account. No friction.

### <Icon name="calendar" /> Scheduling

Life360 tracks location passively and continuously — there's no scheduled check-in concept.

CheckIn More works more like a confirmed alarm. You set a check-in time (or multiple times across the week), choose your window, and check in when the time comes. Paid plans support unlimited check-ins per day and different schedules by day of the week. You can also pause for travel without canceling.

### <Icon name="smile" /> Check-in Methods

Life360 has an SOS button — a one-way emergency signal.

CheckIn More has four check-in modes: a one-tap okay, a mood status, an assistance request, and a combination of both. The mood check-in gives your contacts more context than a binary alive/not-alive signal.

### <Icon name="circle-dollar-sign" /> Price

Life360's free plan offers basic location sharing. The Gold plan — the most popular paid tier — runs $14.99/month and adds emergency dispatch and driving reports.

CheckIn More's free plan is genuinely useful for a single contact. The Plus plan at $6.99/month adds SMS notifications and unlimited check-ins. The Premium plan at $19.99/month adds AI phone calls to contacts when you miss a check-in.

---

## Which One Is Right for You?

**Choose CheckIn More if** you're an adult living alone (or someone who loves one) and want a simple, private, schedule-based daily safety net. Your contacts don't need to install anything.

**Choose Life360 if** you need real-time location sharing across a family group — especially for tracking kids, monitoring teen drivers, or coordinating family logistics. Its crash detection and Gold-tier emergency dispatch are genuinely useful for those scenarios.

They're not really competing for the same user. If you landed here because Life360 felt like the wrong fit, CheckIn More probably is what you were looking for.
