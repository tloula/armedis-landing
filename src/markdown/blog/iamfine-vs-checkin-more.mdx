## The Key Differences

### How Check-ins Work

This is the most fundamental difference between the two services.

**IAmFine calls you.** Each day at your scheduled time, you receive an automated phone call. Press 1 to confirm you're okay. If you miss it, IAmFine tries up to 5 more times over the next hour, then alerts your Care Circle. You can also check in early by calling or texting IAmFine's number, or logging into their website.

**CheckIn More prompts you through the app.** At your scheduled time, you open the app and tap to check in — or share your mood, or request help. No incoming phone call to answer.

Neither approach is objectively better. IAmFine's phone call works on any device and requires no apps. CheckIn More's app gives you more check-in options and scheduling flexibility, but does require a smartphone.

### Scheduling Flexibility

IAmFine lets you set one check-in time per day. That's the full extent of scheduling control.

CheckIn More works more like your smartphone's alarm clock app — you can set different check-in times for different days, enable multiple check-ins per day, choose exactly how long your check-in window stays open before contacts are notified, and pause check-ins for travel without canceling.

### Check-in Depth

IAmFine's check-in is a single signal: did you respond, or didn't you?

CheckIn More has four check-in options: a one-tap okay, a mood status update, an assistance request, and a combination of both. The mood check-in gives your contacts more context about how you're actually feeling — not just that you're alive and answered a call.

### Smartphone Requirement

IAmFine intentionally works without a smartphone. It uses a regular phone call, which means it works on landlines and older cell phones. This is a genuine advantage for seniors who aren't smartphone users.

CheckIn More requires a smartphone to send check-ins. Your contacts don't need the app — they receive alerts by email, SMS, or phone call — but the person checking in does need an iOS or Android device.

### Price

IAmFine has one plan at $14.99/month, billed monthly or $120/year. No free tier.

CheckIn More offers three plans: a free email plan, an SMS plan at $6.99/month, and a Voice plan at $19.99/month. Paid plans include a free trial.

## Which One Is Right for You?

**Choose IAmFine if** your loved one doesn't use a smartphone and is most comfortable with a daily phone call — no apps, no touchscreen. IAmFine's phone-first design makes it a natural fit for that situation.

**Choose CheckIn More if** you want a free starting point, more scheduling flexibility, multiple daily check-ins, or the ability to share mood context with your contacts. It's also the better fit if the person checking in is a smartphone user who'd prefer to initiate the check-in themselves rather than wait for an incoming call.

Both services solve the same core problem — daily confirmation that someone living alone is okay. The right one depends on which device and interaction model fits your life.
