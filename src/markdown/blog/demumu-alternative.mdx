### <Icon name="smartphone" /> iOS Only — Android Users Are Left Out

Demumu is only available on Apple's App Store. If you use an Android device, you're out of luck. CheckIn More is available on both iOS and Android.

When Demumu detects a missed check-in, it sends an **email** to your emergency contact. Email gets ignored. Email goes to spam. Email doesn't wake anyone up at night.

CheckIn More offers email, SMS text, and phone call notifications - formats people actually respond to.

### <Icon name="users" /> Only Two Emergency Contacts

Demumu allows up to two emergency contacts. CheckIn More's paid plans support **unlimited contacts**, each with customizable alert preferences.

### <Icon name="calendar" /> No Schedule Control

Demumu checks in daily. That's it. CheckIn More lets you set check-in times by day of the week, run multiple check-ins per day, and adjust the window before an alert fires.

### <Icon name="globe" /> Built for China, Not for You

Demumu was built quickly for a Chinese audience and is only now attempting a global rollout. CheckIn More was built from day one for the US market — with US-based, founder-led support.
